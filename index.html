Ryan Atallah

Ryan is an entrepreneur, technologist and product designer passionate about designing from first principles to create simple solutions to complex problems. He is currently the CEO & Co-Founder of Spindle, an intelligent No-Code platform that empowers anyone to generate & share business data products quickly. Previously, he was the CTO & Co-Founder of ClearGraph, where he worked to democratize analytics through natural language querying technology, until it was acquired by Tableau in 2017. He studied computer science and design as an undergraduate at Stanford University.
