Ryan Atallah

Ryan is an entrepreneur, technologist and product designer passionate about designing from first principles to create simple solutions to complex problems. He studied computer science and design as an undergraduate at Stanford University. He then was the Co-Founder and CTO at ClearGraph, working to democratize analytics through natural language querying technology, until it was acquired by Tableau in 2017. He's now working on bringing NLP to Tableau, the world's most powerful data visualization & analytics platform.
